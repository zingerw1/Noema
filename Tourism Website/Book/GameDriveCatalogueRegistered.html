Makgadikgadi Pans Exploration

Location: Makgadikgadi Pans, Botswana • Explore prehistoric salt pans and desert wildlife.

Vast, silent, and stunning. This unique terrain promises sightings of meerkats, flamingos, and surreal sunsets across the salt flats.

Highlights

  • Guided exploration of ancient salt pans
  • See meerkats and zebra migrations
  • Stargazing under the clearest skies
  • Cultural storytelling by local San guides

Rates & Availability

Starting from BWP 1,800 per person (includes transport and cultural guide).
