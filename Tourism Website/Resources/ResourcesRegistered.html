Transportation Guide

Getting around Botswana safely and efficiently

Air Travel

  • • Domestic flights between major cities
  • • Charter flights to safari camps
  • • Main hub: Sir Seretse Khama Airport

Road Transport

  • • 4x4 recommended for safaris
  • • Public buses between cities
  • • Taxis available in urban areas

Navigation Tips

  • • Download offline maps
  • • Carry physical maps
  • • Check road conditions

Safety Tips

  • • Drive on the left side
  • • Carry extra fuel
  • • Avoid night driving
